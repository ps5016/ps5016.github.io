This is a net-based art inspired by the everyday objects and discovery of anything related to lost things.
Feel free to click around in this digital archive room,
opening up cabinets, drawers, hovering over items, thinking about things that have part ways with you with or without you noticing.
This is an on going project, planned to be expanded in dynamic ways of demonstrating the concept of lost
